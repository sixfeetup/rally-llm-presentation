AI & DEI: Intersections, Implications and Opportunities
August 30, 2023 | 10:00am – 10:45am
ALL
| Room 241
Join us for an engaging workshop exploring the intersections, implications, and opportunities arising at the confluence of artificial intelligence and diversity, equity, and inclusion. Through thought-provoking presentation will spark a dynamic conversation into the myriad of ways AI technologies hold the potential to both perpetuate and address the obstacles to inclusive innovation, entrepreneurship, and entrepreneurial ecosystems.This workshop is designed to empower participants with valuable insights and practical strategies to navigate the complex relationship between AI and DEI in today’s rapidly evolving technological and political landscape.
