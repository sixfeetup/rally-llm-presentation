Linda Ricci is a globally recognized expert on Immersive Technology / Spatial Computing and the impact of the Metaverse. With 20+ years of experience, she advises executive management teams, helping some of the world’s most successful companies enter, market and monetize transformational and emerging technologies. Most recently delivering global partnerships for Meta’s $150 million Immersive Learning Fund, she is a sought-after speaker / presenter (Metaverse, Web3, XR, WebXR, AR, VR, AI), published author, an avid creator in the XR space, and an award-winning artist. Linda has a degree in Mass Communication from Boston University and an MBA from the Rotterdam School of Management, Erasmus University (NL).
