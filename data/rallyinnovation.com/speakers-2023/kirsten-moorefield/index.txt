Kirsten is the cofounder of Cloverleaf.me — a B2B SaaS platform that provides Automated Coaching™ to tens of thousands of teams in the biggest brands across the globe – where she oversees all things Product and Brand. She often speaks on the power of diversity of thought and psychologically safe cultures, from her TEDx talk, to her podcast “People are Complicated,” her LinkedIn Lives with Talent, Learning and Development Leaders, and her upcoming book “Thrive: A Manifesto for a New Era of Collaboration.” While building Cloverleaf, Kirsten has also been building her young family in Cincinnati, Ohio where she lives with her husband and two young kids.
