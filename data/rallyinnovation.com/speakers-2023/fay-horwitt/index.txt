Fay Horwitt serves as President and CEO of Forward Cities, where she oversees organizational and programmatic strategy. She specializes in the development of collaborative systems and narrative change frameworks for catalyzing more equitable entrepreneurial ecosystems. Fay is a sought after presenter, truth-teller and thought leader on the topic of ecosystem building. She has a personal goal to see the racial wealth gap close in her lifetime and is committed to the deep, multi-facted and collective work it will take to make that happen.
